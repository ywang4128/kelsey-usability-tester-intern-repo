# Agile Ceremonies & Team Collaboration

## What are the main Agile ceremonies, and what is their purpose?
Daily Stand-ups: aligns team, see issues early
Sprint Planning (Scrum) vs. Continuous Prioritization (Kanban): Scrum plans fixed sprint backlog while Kanban pulls ongoing via board as capacity allows, no sprints.
Retrospectives: end of sprint review, what went well, improve. Drives continuous improvement
Backlog Refinement: mid-sprint grooming of stories for clarity, preps future planning

## How do Agile teams collaborate asynchronously and across time zones?
Teams use async tools like Slack or Team update. Overlap 2-3 hrs daily for key syncs and strategy decisions.

## How do Agile ceremonies help with communication and alignment?
They boost communication through regular alignment and feedback, reducing misalignment.
