# Working in an Agile Team

## What do you think are the biggest benefits and challenges of Agile?
Faster feedback loops catch UX issues early, flexibility handles shifting priorities

## How can Agile principles help improve your role and responsibilities?
Test features early, even if they’re not perfect. Give quick feedback and help the team improve the product step by step

## Summarize the main differences between Scrum and Kanban in your own words.
Scrum is time-boxed with ceremonies for teams building new features.
Kanban is flow-based for maintenance and testing—limit work-in-progress, move cards right on a board as done. 

## Identify one Agile principle you think will be most useful in your work.
Simplicity
prioritizing critical bugs over cosmetic ones.
