# Professionalism at Focus Bear

## Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?
- clear roles
- open communication
- mutual respect

## What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?
- chuck messages, for instance, 1 idea at a time
- confirm understanding
- inclusive language

## How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?
celebrate small milestones, keep feedback private, update quick wins to share the joy with the team

## How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?
- go through agenda prior the meeting
- list the things I need to update
- prepare questions or ideas
- test mic and camera

## What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?
- spot issue early
- propose fixes with screenshote
- share resources

## How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?
I'll friendly nudge when following up with colleagues or supervisors.
And if I don't get response over 3 days or deadline nears, I will escalate politely to supervisor.

## What are some things you won't do?
- assume instead of asking for clarification
- gossip about teammates
- send late-night messages without "no rush" disclaimer
