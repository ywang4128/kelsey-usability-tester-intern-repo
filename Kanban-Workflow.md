# Agile Workflows & Kanban

## How does a Kanban board work, and how does it help manage workflow?
It visualize tasks as cards moving through work flow columns, limiting WIP to ensure smooth flow and spot bottlenecks.

## What do the different columns on a Kanban board represent? (e.g., Backlog, In Progress, Blocked, Done)
It represents different status of the work.

## How do tasks move through the board, and who is responsible for updating them?
They move left to right as progressed, assignees update status daily for transparency.

## What are the benefits of limiting work in progress (WIP)?
It can prevent overload and multitasking, and reveals issues.

## How does Kanban help manage priorities and avoid overload?
It prioritizes via pull system, and avoid overload by finishing before starting.
