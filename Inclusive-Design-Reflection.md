# Working with Vulnerable Populations
## How can you adjust your communication style to be more inclusive of neurodivergent users and teammates?
offering multiple channels, using clear and direct language, respecting varied response times, and avoid sarcasm.

## What are some common UX or communication pitfalls that might make Focus Bear less accessible or supportive?
flashing notifications, unpredictable navigation,vague instructions, rigid timers increase cognitive load for AuDHD users.

## What is one practical change you can make in your work to better support vulnerable populations?
As a usability tester and bug hunter at Focus Bear, I could add sensory overload testing to the standard checklist, avoid auto-playing audio, flashing notifications, or cluttered drag-and-drop interfaces during sessions with neurodivergent users.
