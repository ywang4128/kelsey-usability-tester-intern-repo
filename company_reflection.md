# Company Overview & Reflection Assignments

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?
According to the Focus Bear website and the induction session held by the founder Jeremy, I would say the initial motivation of creating this app was Jeremy's love to his wife (hehe).  
He wanted to stay focused on his life and show his determination to building a life with her. Focus Bear was also created by a neurodivergent team to solve the problem that most traditional productivity apps don't work well for people with AuDHD.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?
- distractions across apps, websites, notifications
- poor routines or habbits in the morning and before bed
- Difficulty staying on task
- being late

## Why do you think this mission is important?
This mission is important because it supports not only people  diagnosed with AuDHD but also anyone who struggles with focus and productivity, which can somehow impact school work, personal life, relationship, health, and etc. People facing these challenges may feel frustrated and depressed. Focus Bear could really make meaningful changes by helping them get back on track with their plans and life, reducing school dropouts, unemployeement, and even suicide. 

## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?
Focus Bear aligns with my values of supporting others, offering encouragement, and helping maintain engagement in daily life. I've been working with a client who has high-functioning autism. In my role, I provide social companionship and help with small goal-setting in a gentle, flexible way. Focus Bear’s approach of supporting focus and productivity without forcing rigid routines reflects the kind of supportive environment I believe in.

I also really like the "Science Behind Focus Bear" session of the website. It explains the design logic of Focus Bear, made me feel more familiar with how human's brain work, really evidence-based and interesting.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?
i personally get easily distracted by social media or notifications, so i've tried several kinds of productivity app but none of them kept me using them consistently.
And also working as a remote companion for the client with Autism, I've seen how difficult it is for someone like them to maintain focus and follow through on goals. This experience gives me a firsthand perspective on why tools like Focus Bear are so valuable.
